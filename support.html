Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new team?
Navigate to the Team Generator page, add your players with their positions, and click "Generate Teams" to create balanced teams automatically.
Can I save my team configurations?
Yes! You can save your team setups and player lists for future use. Simply use the save feature in the Team Generator.
How do I track match statistics?
Use the Match Tracker feature to record goals, assists, and other match events. All data is automatically saved to player profiles.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We take data security seriously. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
