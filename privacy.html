Privacy Policy

At NiteRun, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our football team management platform.

Last Updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using NiteRun, including:

Personal Information:
Name, email address, phone number, and profile information when you create an account or update your profile.
Football-Related Data:
Player positions, skills, match statistics, team compositions, and performance metrics that you input into the platform.
Usage Information:
How you interact with our platform, including features used, pages visited, and time spent on different sections.
Technical Information:
Device information, IP address, browser type, and operating system for security and optimization purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Purpose Description
Service Provision To provide and maintain our football team management services
Team Generation To create balanced teams using our algorithms and your input data
Performance Tracking To analyze and display player and team statistics
Communication To send important updates, support responses, and service notifications
Improvement To enhance our platform features and user experience
Security To protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

With Your Consent:
When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information with other users or third parties.

With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform (hosting, analytics, customer support).
Legal Requirements:
When required by law, court order, or government request to protect our rights and safety.
Business Transfers:
In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, where user information may be transferred as part of the business.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption:
All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
Access Controls:
Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms to ensure only authorized personnel can access your data.
Regular Audits:
Regular security assessments and penetration testing to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
Data Backup:
Secure backup systems to protect against data loss and ensure business continuity.
